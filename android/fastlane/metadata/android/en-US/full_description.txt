Peteks is a beautifully designed note-taking app with a warm, cozy aesthetic.

✏️ Rich text editor — bold, italic, headings, checklists, and more
📁 Folders & tags to keep notes organised
🔒 Secure notes protected by pattern or password
☁️ Google Drive backup & restore
🌙 Comfy Light and Comfy Dark themes
📅 Calendar view for date-based browsing
🎤 Voice recordings and image attachments
🔍 Full-text search across all your notes

Perfect for journaling, task lists, study notes, and daily ideas.
