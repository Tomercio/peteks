Privacy Policy — Peteks

Last updated: May 2026

Peteks ("the App") is a personal notes application. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Notes content — Stored locally on your device using Hive. Not transmitted to any server by default.
  • Google Account information — If you use the Google Drive backup feature, the App accesses your Google account name and email solely to authenticate with Google Drive. We do not store or share this information.
  • Google Drive files — With your permission, the App creates a folder called "Peteks Notes" in your Google Drive to store your note backups. The App only accesses files it creates.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data.
  • We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers.
  • We do not transmit your notes to any third-party server.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, or any other device data.

3. Security

Secure notes are protected using HMAC-SHA256 hashing with a unique per-note salt. Passwords and patterns are never stored in plain text.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase — analytics (anonymous crash reporting).
  • Google Sign-In / Google Drive API — only used when you choose to back up your notes.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact
